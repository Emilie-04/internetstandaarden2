Episodes

Episode 1: The Zodiac Killer

In the late 1960s, a shadowy figure known as the Zodiac Killer terrorized Northern California, leaving behind a trail of cryptic letters, unsolved ciphers, and brutal murders. While officially linked to five killings, the Zodiac claimed responsibility for dozens more, taunting law enforcement and the media in a deadly game of cat and mouse. Despite extensive investigations, the killer was never caught, and the case remains one of the most chilling unsolved mysteries in American history. Join us as we dive deep into the dark legacy of the Zodiac Killer—his victims, his puzzles, and the ongoing quest for justice.

Episode 3: Jon Benet Ramsey

The JonBenét Ramsey case is one of the most infamous and enduring unsolved mysteries in the U.S. True crime enthusiasts and investigators alike have speculated about what happened on Christmas night, 1996, in Boulder, Colorado, when six-year-old beauty queen JonBenét was found murdered in her family's home.

Episode 4: Jack The Ripper

This true crime podcast delves into the infamous case of Jack the Ripper, a serial killer who terrorized the Whitechapel district of London in 1888. The Ripper targeted women, mostly prostitutes, brutally murdering and mutilating at least five victims, known as the "Canonical Five."

Episode 5: Tupac Shakur

Tupac Shakur, one of the most influential rappers of all time, was tragically gunned down in a drive-by shooting on September 7, 1996, in Las Vegas.

Episode 6: Natalie Woods

Natalie Wood was a beloved Hollywood actress known for her roles in films like West Side Story and Rebel Without a Cause. However, her untimely death in 1981 remains one of Hollywood’s most enduring mysteries. Wood drowned near Santa Catalina Island while on a yacht with her husband, actor Robert Wagner, and fellow actor Christopher Walken.
